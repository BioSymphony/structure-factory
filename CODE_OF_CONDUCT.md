# Code Of Conduct

BioSymphony Structure Factory follows a simple standard: be respectful, evidence-oriented, and careful with safety, privacy, and scientific claims.

Participants should:

- Treat contributors and users with professional respect.
- Keep discussions focused on reproducible evidence and clear claim boundaries.
- Avoid sharing private biological data, credentials, license files, or provider secrets in issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- State uncertainty plainly when a result is computational, partial, blocked, or insufficient.

Maintainers may moderate or remove content that is abusive, unsafe, privacy-invasive, or misleading about biological or clinical claims.

Report conduct or security concerns through the channels in `SECURITY.md`.
